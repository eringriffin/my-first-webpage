Erin's favorite places on earth

A trip around the world

Kauai

Kauai is the oldest of the Hawaiian islands and known for its impressive coastlines, as well as being one of the wettest spots on earth. With more wild chickens than people, picturesque beaches, and beautiful hiking options, it's the perfect location for a relaxing vacation!

What I love:

  • Foods: Poke, Spam Musubi
  • Activities: Beach time, hiking, boating the Napali coast.

New Orleans

A city older than the United States, New Orleans (NOLA for short) is a place unlike anywhere else on earth. An amalgamation of French, Spanish, and Creole cultures have created a vibrant city known for its unique architecture, decadent food, and rich musical scene. If you visit, you may just have the time of your life!

What I love:

  • Foods: Seafood Gumbo, Po Boys, Crawfish Etoufee.
  • Activities: Jazz parades, listening to live music on Frenchman Street, visiting the cemetaries.

Ubud

Ubud is a truly magical place that capitvates almost everyone who visits. Famous for its temples, rice paddies, and wild monkeys, it's the perfect place for someone looking for a spiritual getaway.

What I love:

  • Foods: Babi Guling, Papaya Salad
  • Activities: Visiting the temples, biking the countryside, viewing Balinese handicrafts and artwork
